Princess Bubblegum

Princess Bubblegum is the incarnation of the Candy Elemental. She rules over the Candy Kingdom, whose population is entirely made out of candy. While Princess Bubblegum is typically kind, good-hearted and well-mannered, she can be very temperamental and has shown a malicious aspect of her personality whenever she is crossed.

  • A Total Nerd

    As a millionaire nerd enthusiast, Princess Bubblegum immerses herself ni every branch of geekdom from rocket science to turtle farming. Her immense knowledge on all things and vigilant dedication to the many fields of science and mathematics, provide an endless supply of interesting projects for Finn and Jake to get excited about.

A Leader

Bubblegum also adores the people of her kingdom and puts their safety above all else. In her dedication, she sometimes drives herself to exhaustion with her tasks. She is also a rational thinker with skepticism towards magic, labeling it as "scientific principles presented like mystical hoodoo." She also claims that "all magic is science," and Wizards just don't know what they're doing so they call it magic.
